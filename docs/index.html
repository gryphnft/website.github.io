We will fund this project from the initial mint. Some monies from mint will be used for marketing, promotions, upgrading tech, paying for discord moderators, and creating new utilities that will be in the smart contracts.

WHAT IS THE UTILITY?

Creator fees are given to Gryph holders evenly as rewards for passively staking. The rest of the utility will be found on the smart contracts. We will be dropping hints along the way and it's up to you to discover them all.

IF I SELL MY GRYPH, CAN I STILL REDEEM?

Once you sell your Gryph, you no longer have the rights to redeem it's rewards (it passes to the new owner).

HOW CAN I REDEEM MY REWARDS?

Current holders of Gryph Legends can redeem their reward in the treasury or directly from the contract. Once the rewards are redeemed the NFT is burned. So you have a choice. You can hold the NFT to upgrade, hold, sale or trade OR you collect the rewards therefore the Griffin is burned.

WHAT DO I NEED TO KNOW BEFORE OWNING A GRYPH?

Rewards are driven by sales volume. No volume means no rewards.
